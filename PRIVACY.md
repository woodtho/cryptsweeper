# Privacy Policy for Cryptsweeper

**Effective date:** July 20, 2026
**Developer:** Thomas Wood ("we", "us", "the developer")

Thank you for playing **Cryptsweeper** (the "Game"). This policy explains how the
Game handles information. The short version: **the Game does not collect, transmit,
sell, or share any personal information.** The developer never receives your locally
saved game data.

---

## 1. Summary

- We do **not** collect personal information.
- We do **not** require an account, sign-in, or registration.
- The Game does **not** send any data off your device. It has **no analytics, no
  advertising, no tracking, no crash reporting, and no in-app purchases.**
- The only information the Game stores is your game progress and settings, saved
  **locally on your own device**. We never see it.

## 2. Information we do not collect

We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit any of the following:

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact details.
- Precise or approximate location.
- Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or files.
- Advertising identifiers or device identifiers used for tracking.
- Usage analytics, behavioral data, or crash logs.
- Payment or financial information.

The Game requests no sensitive device permissions (no location, camera,
microphone, contacts, or storage-access permissions) to play.

## 3. Data stored on your device

To let you play, save your progress, and keep your preferences, the Game stores a
small amount of data **locally on your device** (using standard on-device app
storage / browser `localStorage`). This data is **not accessible to us**. It includes:

- **Game progress and unlocks** — your run state, saved games, daily-challenge
  records, statistics, and Delver/achievement progression.
- **Collection records** — which enemies, items, and cards you have discovered.
- **Settings and preferences** — sound and music on/off, motion and contrast
  options, icon and display choices, and similar in-game options.

You are always in control of this data (see **Section 6 — Retention and deletion**).
Your operating system or platform may include this local data in a device backup if
you have backups enabled. Those backups are controlled by you and the platform
provider; we cannot access them.

## 4. No third parties

The Game does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or
social SDKs. The installed mobile Game does not make network requests during normal
play. No third party receives your information from us through the Game.

If you downloaded the Game from an app store or platform (for example Google Play,
the Apple App Store, or a web host), that platform operates independently under its
own privacy policy and may process information when you download, back up, or access
the Game (such as account, device, IP address, request, backup, and download records).
That processing is governed by the platform, not by us. Please review the relevant
platform's privacy policy:

- Google Play / Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Apple App Store / Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## 5. Network access

The Game is designed to run entirely offline and does not need an internet
connection to play. If an app build technically includes standard network
permission (as some app frameworks do by default), the Game does **not** use it to
collect, transmit, or share your personal information.

## 6. Retention and deletion

Because all Game data is stored locally on your device and not on any server:

- **We retain nothing about you** — there is no account or server-side profile to
  delete.
- You can delete all locally stored Game data at any time by:
  - using any in-Game option to delete or overwrite saves, and/or
  - clearing the app's data/storage in your device settings, and/or
  - uninstalling the Game (which removes its local data on most platforms), or
  - clearing your browser's site data if you play a web version.

## 7. Children's privacy

The Game is suitable for a general audience and **does not knowingly collect any
personal information from anyone, including children.** Because the Game collects no
personal data and shows no advertising, it does not build profiles of, or target,
children or any other users. If you believe a child has somehow provided us personal
information, note that we hold none — but you may contact us with any concern.

## 8. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU/UK GDPR
or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including rights to access,
correct, or delete personal information, and not to be discriminated against for
exercising them. Because the Game **collects no personal information and we hold no
data about you**, there is generally nothing for us to access, correct, delete, or
sell — and **we do not sell or share personal information.** You can still contact
us with any question or request (Section 10).

## 9. Security

We do not receive or store your information, so there is nothing for us to secure on
our side. Data saved by the Game remains on your own device and is protected by your
device's own security. Please keep your device secure to protect any locally saved
data.
