## Full description

Descend into the Undermine, where every battle is a Minesweeper board and every card gives you another way to control the odds.

CryptSweeper combines deduction, turn-based combat, and deckbuilding. Read numbered tiles, mark hidden mines, and reveal safe ground to damage the creatures stalking each floor. When certainty runs out, spend Energy on cards that scan, reshape, protect, and exploit the board.

BUILD YOUR DELVER
Choose a delver with a distinct starting kit and passive ability. Add cards, trinkets, and gadgets as the descent unfolds, then adapt your build to the threats below.

SURVIVE THE CRYPT
Fight regular enemies, dangerous elites, and multi-stage bosses across three haunted strata. Camps offer a moment to recover and prepare. The rat merchant trades useful tools for hard-earned gold.

SOLVE STRANGE ROOMS
Discover a large catalog of events inspired by probability, logic, game theory, and classic puzzles. Later depths introduce tougher no-guess Minesweeper boards, Sudoku, word squares, sequences, Lights Out, and nonograms.

MASTER EVERY DESCENT
Unlock delvers through play, discover hundreds of cards and items, fill enemy and collection indexes, and return for seeded daily challenges.

Features:
• Minesweeper deduction fused with strategic card combat
• 10 unlockable delvers with different play styles
• 200 cards plus trinkets and gadgets
• 100 events and a growing set of logic puzzles
• Three strata, elite encounters, and boss battles
• Daily seeded challenges and persistent discovery indexes
• Designed mobile-first with touch controls and portrait play
• Playable offline

Every safe tile is a plan. Every mine is a weapon. How deep can you dig?
