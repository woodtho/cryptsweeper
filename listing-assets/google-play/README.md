# Google Play listing assets

The files in this directory are prepared to match Google Play's current image dimensions. Upload only the device categories the release actually supports.

## Ready to upload

- `app-icon-512.png` — 512 × 512 PNG, under 1 MB
- `feature-graphic-1024x500.png` — 1024 × 500 opaque PNG
- `phone/` — 1080 × 1920 portrait screenshots
- `tablet-7/` — 1080 × 1920 portrait screenshots rendered at a tablet viewport
- `tablet-10/` — 1600 × 2560 portrait screenshots rendered at a tablet viewport
- `pc/logo-600x400.png` — transparent 600 × 400 PNG
- `pc/feature-graphic-1920x1080.jpg` — text-free 16:9 cover art
- `pc/screenshots/` — 1920 × 1080 screenshots
- `chromebook/` — 1920 × 1080 screenshots

Do not upload Chromebook, Google Play Games on PC, or Android XR assets unless those device form factors are enabled and the game has been tested there. A promotional video is optional and is not generated here.

`source/` contains the high-resolution generated masters. `listing-copy.md` contains the copy and suggested screenshot captions.
